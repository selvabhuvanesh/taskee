Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

taskoot ("we", "our", or "the app") is a family task management app built by Selva Bhuvanesh. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Apple, we collect:

  • Name (first and last name)
  • Email address
  • Apple User ID (a unique identifier)

You may choose to hide your email using Apple's private relay service.

Family & Task Data

When you use the app, we store:

  • Task details (name, due date, assigned person, reward amount, status)
  • Family member profiles (name, role, avatar)
  • Chat messages within your family group
  • Shopping list items
  • Reward redemption requests
  • Coin/earnings totals

Device Data

We store locally on your device:

  • Notification history (up to 100 recent notifications)
  • Subscription status and preferences
  • App settings and onboarding state

How We Use Your Information

All data is used solely to provide the app's functionality:

  • Account info identifies you within your family group
  • Task and family data enables task assignment, tracking, and rewards
  • Notifications remind you about upcoming tasks and family activity

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or analytics.

Data Storage & Security

  • On-device data is stored in iOS-encrypted storage (UserDefaults and local files)
  • Synced data is stored in Apple CloudKit (iCloud), Apple's cloud infrastructure, within a private container accessible only to your family group
  • Purchases are processed entirely through Apple's App Store (StoreKit 2)

We do not operate our own servers. All cloud data is managed by Apple's infrastructure under Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

We use only Apple's first-party services:

ServicePurpose
Sign in with AppleAuthentication
Apple CloudKit (iCloud)Family data sync
Apple StoreKit 2In-app subscriptions
Apple Push NotificationsTask reminders & alerts

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your family data is shared only with members of your family group through Apple CloudKit's shared database zones.

Children's Privacy

taskoot is designed for family use, including children. Children join a family group managed by a parent. We collect only the minimum information needed (name and avatar) and do not collect children's email addresses or Apple IDs independently. All children's data is managed within the parent's family group.

Data Retention & Deletion

  • You can delete your account and all associated data by removing yourself from the family group and deleting the app
  • Local data is removed when the app is uninstalled
  • CloudKit data can be managed through your iCloud account settings at icloud.com

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by discontinuing use of the app

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
